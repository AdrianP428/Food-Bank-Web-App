About Us

Western Mass Food Banks is a community-driven network dedicated to reducing hunger and strengthening food security across Western Massachusetts. Our goal is simple — to ensure every household has access to nutritious food.

Our Mission

We believe that food is a basic human right. Through partnerships with local pantries, farms, and organizations, we collect and distribute healthy food to those in need. Our mission extends beyond food access — we aim to foster dignity, community, and hope.

What We Do

  • Operate food banks and pantries across Western Massachusetts
  • Collaborate with farms and grocers to reduce food waste
  • Organize volunteer programs to pack and distribute meals
  • Advocate for policies that address the root causes of hunger

Our Impact

Each year, we distribute thousands of pounds of food through our network, serving individuals and families in need. With your support, we continue to grow our reach and impact more lives every day.
